Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out? +

Check-in time is at 3:00 PM and check-out time is at 12:00 PM.

Do you offer airport shuttle service? +

Yes, we offer airport shuttle service for an additional fee. Please contact our concierge desk to arrange for this service.

Is parking available at the hotel? +

Yes, we offer valet parking for our guests for an additional fee. Self-parking is not available at our hotel.

Do you have a fitness center? +

Yes, we have a fully equipped fitness center available for our guests. The fitness center is open 24 hours a day and offers state-of-the-art equipment and personal training services for an additional fee.

Do you offer room service? +

Yes, we offer 24-hour room service for our guests. Our menu features a wide range of delicious options, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and late-night snacks. Simply call our room service team to place your order and we'll take care of the rest.
